Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Zaika Express

What is the delivery time? ▶
Delivery usually takes 30-45 minutes depending on your location and traffic conditions. You will get live updates once your order is confirmed.
What payment methods do you accept? ▶
We accept UPI, Debit/Credit cards, Net Banking, and secure gateways like Razorpay and PayPal.
Can I cancel my order? ▶
Orders once confirmed cannot be canceled. However, in case of any issues, please contact our support team immediately.
Do you offer refunds? ▶
Refunds are applicable only for incorrect or incomplete orders. Please report the issue within 24 hours of delivery.
Is there a minimum order value? ▶
Yes, the minimum order value is ₹150 for delivery. There is no minimum order for self-pickup.
